Game Rules

Welcome to Snap Game! The rules are simple:

  1. Players take turns playing cards from their hands.
  2. If two consecutive cards match, the first player to shout "SNAP!" wins both piles.
  3. The game continues until one or more players run out of cards.
  4. The player(s) with the most points at the end of the game wins.
